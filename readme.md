brief explanation of how my code works
Step 1: The user feeds his or her data (all required credentials)

Step 2: The information is directly send to my database from where I analyze the skills manually

step 3: I then read through the skills and expectations 

step 4: The user can also go through the available jobs in the sample page and apply direct

step 5: I then sent an email or an sms text to them telling them about the available opportunities closest to them
and how to apply for one

No authentication is required or login

It is an open source for the general public
